Basic Package Details

The Basic package is ideal for beginners or those who want access to the gym without any frills. You will have access during working hours and can follow a basic exercise program provided by our trainers. Although the support from the trainer is basic, it’s enough to get you started on your fitness journey.

Premium Package Details

The Premium package offers more personalized attention, including a custom training program tailored to your needs. You also have free access to group trainings and the ability to consult with our in-house nutritionist, which can help you optimize your diet for better results.

VIP Package Details

The VIP package is our most comprehensive offering, including private training sessions with a personal trainer. You also enjoy unlimited access to our spa center and as many group lessons as you wish, making it perfect for those who want the best in fitness and relaxation.
